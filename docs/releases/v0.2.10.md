# KoalaBye v0.2.10

This release prepares KoalaBye's public uninstall survey pages for production use.

## Highlights

- Privacy-preserving 30-minute deduplication prevents repeated campaign visits from being counted twice.
- Campaign attribution continues without storing IP addresses, raw user agents, or other direct identifiers.
- Privacy and imprint pages now provide clearer operator guidance and a configurable public contact email.
- A redesigned shared footer appears consistently across public, authenticated, and legal pages.
- The footer includes project branding, Ko-fi, legal pages, MIT license, GitHub, and the current build identifier.
- Favicon PNGs and the web app manifest are generated reproducibly from the existing SVG logo.
- The light/dark mode toggle icon remains visible and consistently sized in both themes.
